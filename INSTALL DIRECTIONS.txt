1. copy the following folders to your garrysmod directory (overwrite when it asks you to)
	-gamemodes
	-maps
	-particles
	-data
2. Subscribe to the following workshop packs:
	https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=488250410	
3. Start up the gamemode gmstranded on the map gms_coastal_island_v2.bsp
4. Enjoy!



*** The hammer_files folder is just the hammer files for some of the maps I've made for stranded if you want to look at them or modify them. They use materials from the workshop packs though, so you might need to extract those into your gmod folder to see them in hammer.
